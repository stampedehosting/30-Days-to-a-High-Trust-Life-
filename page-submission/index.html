The Contributing Author Page Process

Here are the steps and guidelines for submitting your content to the Art of Connection book series.

The Submission Process

  1. Payment

    The author pays for the page through ThriveCart.

  2. Instruction Email & Submission

    The author receives an automated email with the following instructions and a submission link to the “Unedited Drive” (including a 30-day timeframe):

    Submission Link (Google Form)

    Quick look: Instruction Email Quick look PDF LINK

    Content Guidelines:

    • Full Name only: No honorariums except in the Author Contact Area for consistency.
    • No “ALL CAPS” in any portion of the page.
    • No special formatting or structures for “added emphasis”.
    • No text directed to any form of selling or commercialism.
    • Quote: 200-character limit (no exceptions).
    • Thought: Must be between 800-1200 characters.
    • Question(s): One or two questions with a total 200-character limit.
    • Square Color Author picture (will be in grayscale for printed books).
    • Author Logo for inclusion into a Dynamic QR code.

    Note: The page is created to project personal thoughts on the Resilience topic.

    In the Author Contact Area: One social media link only; tags to fit with a 50-character limit.

    Page Submission Contribution: Author contributions are DUE 14 Days from the date of payment.

  3. Submission Review

    The submitted work is reviewed by the editor within two weeks.

  4. Editing Process

    • Edits Required: If edits are needed, the edited copy is sent back to the author for approval within three working days (text only, not image formatting).
    • Edits NOT required: The author is notified of the approval.

  5. Post-Approval

    Once the editor and author approve, the page is stored in the “Edited Drive.” Any further edits will be charged at the rates below:

    • Post-Approval Content Contact Edits: $100 minimum charge for all page edits.
    • Post-Approval Author Information Edits: $50 minimum charge for all author information edits.
    • Post-Approval Author image and QR Code Edits: $40 minimum charge for all author image edits.

  6. Author Date Selection

    Concurrent with author approval, the author receives a notification email with the VOL 6 – 2026 Requested Date Form and a Calendar link, https://teamup.com/ks1rqax6skf8p8ii94, with the open dates from which to choose.

Date Reconciliation: Once the author selects a date, it is reconciled with the public calendar and reserved accordingly.

NOTE: Pages Purchase are non-refundable after 30 days. Page not submitted within 60 days of purchase, will no longer be valid or accepted.
